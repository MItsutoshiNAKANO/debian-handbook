A.11. DoudouLinux

DoudouLinux targets young children (starting from 2 years old). To achieve this goal, it provides an heavily customized graphical interface (based on LXDE) and comes with many games and educative applications. Internet access is filtered to prevent children from visiting problematic websites. Advertisements are blocked. The goal is that parents should be free to let their children use their computer once booted into DoudouLinux. And children should love using DoudouLinux, just like they enjoy their gaming console.
→ http://www.doudoulinux.org
